Matthew Corey is a Republican candidate for the U.S. Senate from Connecticut. Corey is running in the general election on
    November 6, 2018, after advancing from the primary on August 14, 2018.

        Corey enlisted in the U.S. Navy following his high school graduation. Following his military service, he worked for the U.S.
    Postal Service and then as a truck driver for the Teamsters (Roadway Corporation). Corey started a high-rise window cleaning
    company, Advanced Services International, in 1990. He then opened McKinnon's Irish Pub in 2002.
